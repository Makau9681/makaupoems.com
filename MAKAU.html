Scared of Love

This time I would be broken.
Actually, broken is too undermining.
But a shattered glass can't break,
Cause it's only glass pieces left.
Grief no longer knows me,
Neither does pain,
For I'm getting used to
Becoming a normalcy.
Lessons learned from different teachers.

Journey of Hearts

Paths we’ve crossed, worlds apart,
Guided by love, a mapless heart.
Through storms and sun, we wander free,
In search of what we’re meant to be.
With every step, our spirits blend,
A journey of hearts, until the end.
